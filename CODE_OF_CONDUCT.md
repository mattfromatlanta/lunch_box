## Code of Conduct

Chompi Pack is a small project maintained by one person, in my spare time, because I enjoy it. There's no team, no formal process, and no professional obligation.

If you want to get involved, I'd be thrilled. Send me a note. I welcome constructive criticism, bug reports, and thoughtful contributions. I have no patience for bad behavior. Harassment, spamming, personal attacks, or bad-faith engagement of any kind will not be indulged. I reserve the right to block or remove anyone at my discretion.

The code of conduct reflects the scale of the project, and may change as the project grows. Life is short. Please be nice. 

*Contact: chompi.pack@matthew.land*
